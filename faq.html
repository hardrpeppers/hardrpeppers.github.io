What is CN Supply Co.?

CN Supply Co. is a research-focused storefront that presents products with clear documentation, transparent communication, and Certificate of Analysis access by request before ordering.

What does "Research Use Only" mean?

Research Use Only means products are intended strictly for laboratory and analytical research purposes. They are not intended for human consumption.

Why are COAs provided?

COAs are provided to help verify batch documentation and maintain transparency, so customers can review product testing records before placing an order.

How do I order?

Open the Products page and use Text to Order to launch your messaging app with prefilled product details, or use Copy Order Details and paste the message into your preferred app.

How does shipping work?

Shipping details are confirmed directly through text after your order message is received. You will provide your name and shipping state during order confirmation.

How do I view a product COA?

Use the View COA button on product cards or visit the COAs page to review available documentation and downloads.

How long does it take to receive a response after texting?

Response times can vary based on message volume, but most order inquiries are answered as quickly as possible in the order they are received.
